Executive Summary

Your current protocol (0.5mm microneedling + 5% minoxidil) fails on two fronts: (1) 40% of minoxidil users lack sufficient follicular sulfotransferase enzyme activity (genetic, not compliance-related), and (2) 0.5mm microneedling targets only the epidermis, missing the dermal papilla (0.7-1.5mm depth) where growth signaling occurs.   • Dosing: 1mg daily, same time
  • Mechanism: Removes DHT pressure on follicles while tretinoin + minoxidil "pull" toward growth
  • Combined effect: Finasteride + minoxidil = 94% improvement vs. 59% minoxidil alone
